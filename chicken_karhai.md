# Chicken Karhai

## Ingrediants
* Whole Chicken (Skinless) (cut in small pieces: 12 -16)
* Black Pepper -- crushed 1 tsp
* White Pepper -- crushed 1 tsp
* Small Cardamom --  5
* Large Cardamom --  1
* Zeera -- 1 1/2 tsp
* Red Chilli Powder - 1 1/2 tsp
* Salt 1 tsp
* Kasoori Mehi 1/2 tsp
* Coriander Dried Leaves (Or Coriander Seeds) -- crushed 2 tsp
* Tomatoes -- 6-7
* Green Chillies -- 8
* Yougut -- 1/4 - 1/3 cup
* Ginger Garlic Paste (1 tsp + 1 1/2 tsp)

## Steps
1. Fry Chicken in oil on high heat 
2. Add ginger garlic paste and fry until chicken changes color to a little brown
3. Add tomatoes (sliced in two pieces) and fry and cover until soft and remove skin using a tongs 
4. Then crush tomoatoes with the tongs and mix
5. Add cut green chillies and fix
6. Add Maslay
7. Add kasori methi
8. Add corinder
9. Add some big green chilies
10. Add julline ginger

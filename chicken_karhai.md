# Chicken Karhai

## Ingrediants
* Whole Chicken (Skinless) (cut in small pieces: 12 -16)
* Black Pepper -- crushed 1 tsp
* White Pepper -- crushed 1 tsp
* Small Cardamom -- crushed 5
* Large Cardamom -- crushed 1
* Zeera -- 1 1/2 tsp
* Red Chilli Powder - 1 1/2 tsp
* Salt 1 tsp
* Coriander -- crushed 2 tsp
* Tomatoes -- 6-7
* Green Chillies -- 8
* Yougut -- 1/4 - 1/3 cup
* Ginger Garlic Paste (1 tsp + 1 1/2 tsp)

## Steps
1. Fry Chicken in oil on high heat until  it changes color

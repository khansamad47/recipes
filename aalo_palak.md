# Allo Palak

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2yi7d0KGZas

## Ingrediants
* 1 Cup Oil
* 2-6 cloves Garic (Lessan) chopped
* Curry Pata 1 table spoon?
* 1 tsp zeera
* 2 tomatoes small
* 1/2 tsp haldi
* 1 tsp red chilli powder
* 1 tsp coriander powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp chilli flakes
* 1 kg Palak
* 1 kg allo
* Some Choti Methi
* Green chili 4-6
* 1/2 cup yougut

## Steps
1. In one cup oil add the garlic, curry leaves 1 tsp zeera
2. Once garlic is red add chopped tomatoes and try till soft
3. Add haldi, red chilii, coriander powder, salt, chilliflakes
4. Add wet chopped palak and some water and cover and cook until they are like a paste
5. Add BOILED potatoes chopped potatoes (boiled for 2-3 minutes)
6. Also add methi and green chilli
7. add yougut
8. add some lemon
9. cover and cook until done.

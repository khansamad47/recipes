# Chicken Stroganoff

## Ingredients
* Boneless Chicken - thin bite-size cuts 600g
* Garlic Powder 1 tsp
* Paprika 1/2 tsp
* Black Pepper 1/2 tsp
* Salt 1/2 tsp

* 40g butter 
* Onions (Finally Chopped) 1 medium
* Mushrooms - 3/4 cup (thin slices)
* Garlic 8 cloves - Minced
* 1-2 tsp black pepper (as per taste)
* 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
* Chicken Stock 1 cube in 2 cups of boiling water
* Heavy whipping cream 1/2 - 3/4 cup
* Worcestershire 1 tbsp
* Sour Cream 1/4 cup

## Steps
1. Mix Boneless Chicken and garlic powder, paprika, black pepper and salt and marinate for an hour
2. Stir fry chicken in 1tsp butter and oil and remove
3. Heat butter and add onions and fry until translucent add garlic fry until light golden
4. Add the mushrooms and fry until dry
5. Add flour and mix for 1-2 mins
6. Add chicken stalk and bring to simmer
7. Add heavy whipping cream cook
8. Add Worcestershire sauce and mix
9. add black pepper
10. Add gravy to sour cream to thin it down
11. add the mixture back to the pan and bring it to simmer while constantly stirring it
12. Add salt or black pepper as needed

# Tehari Beef and Allo
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KVKz6Q1gAmo

## Ingredients
* Oil 100ml 
* 4 Medium Potatoes
* Whole Black Masala
  - Green cardamom 4
  - Black pepper 1 & 1/2 tsp
  - Cinnamon stick 1
  - Cloves 1/2 tsp
  - Mace flower 1/2
  - Black cardamom 2
  - Cumin seeds 1&1/2 tbsp
  - Star anise 1
* 3 bay leafs
* 600 grams beef -- washed and drained
* Onion 2s ( medium/small size) sliced
* Salt 1 tsp | 2tsp (for )
* Turmeic powder 1 tsp
* red chili powder 1 tsp
* coriander powder 2 tsp
* garam masala 1/2 tsp
* 3 tbsp garlic ginger paste
* 2-3 tbsp yougut
* 5 green chillis
* 600 grams rice -- washed and soaked

## Steps
1. Add oil to cooker and fry the potatoes and remove
2. Add the whole black masala and let crackle for 15 -20 seconds and bay leaves and let fry for 
   few more seconds
3. Add **one of the** sliced onions and fry until golden brown
4. Add meat and fry on medium heat until changes color (2 mins)
5. Add Salt 1 tsp, turmeric, red chili coriander, garam masla powder and stir for few seconds on low heat
6. Add tbsp ginger garlic paste and mix and add very small sliced onion and mix this well
7. Add water to cook meat on low heat (1 cup) and cover and cook until tender
8. Once meat is done bun it until oil is separate then add yougut and cook until oil again seperates
9. Add the fried potatoes and green chillis and mix, add rice which was soaked
10. Add water such that total water is 2 times the rice (cups) -- or 1 inch above the rice
11. Add salt 2tsp salt ( 1tsp per 250 gram rice) and daniya powdina leaves and kerwra water.
12. Cook on high heat first and then low heat until rice are done

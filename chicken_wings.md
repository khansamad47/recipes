# Ingrediants
* Onion powder 1 tsp
* Garlic powder 1 tsp
* Ginger pownder 1 tsp
* Smoked Paprika 1 tsp
* Cumin powder 1 tsp
* Black Pepper 1 tsp
* Salt

## Steps
1. Apply seasoning and dry brine on a rack overnight in the fridge (12hours)
2. *Optional* Apply some all-purpose flour (for extra crispness) 
3. Spray oil on the air fryer tray and place wings with spaces between with skin side up (so fat renders on the wing)
4. Run for 400F for 8-12 mins or 380F for 15 mins

## Sauce Toss
* Butter
* Hotsauce
* Honey
* gochuchang
* Spicy noodle sauce

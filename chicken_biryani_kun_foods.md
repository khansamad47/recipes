## Ingredients: KORMA
* Chicken 1kg
* Oil 1 cup oil
* Tomato 500kg
* Ginger Garlic Paste 1tbsp
* Green Chillies Paste 1tbsp

* Salt 1 tbsp for korma
* Red Chilli powder 1 tbsp

* Dried Plum 8-10
* Yougut 1 1/2 cup
* Potato 1/2 kg


### Use whole spices:
* Cumin whole 1 tbsp
* Black Pepper Corns whole 1 tbsp
* Long 5-6 pieces
* Star Anise Whole 1 flower
* Green Cardamom whole 5pcs
* Cinamon Sticks whole 2pcs

### Grind these spices
* Cumin 1 tbsp
* Cinamon sticks 2 small
* Nutmeg small piece
* Mace 1 small pieces

* Oil 100gms for dum
* Lemon Slices 2lemons
* Green Mint Leaves
* Green Coriander Leaves

## Steps
1. Heat 1 cup oil and 1/2 kg tomatoes until soft
2. Add gingergarlic paste and green chili paste and cook for 4-5 mins
3. Add black whole spices and mix for few minutes
4. Add salt and red chilli
5. Add dried plums
6. Add 3 cups of water and the grinded spices mix and then add yougut and boil
7. Add potatoes 1/2 kg and cover and cook for 7-8 minutes 
8. Patatoes 1/2 cooked and still had water add chicken cook 10 -12 minutes

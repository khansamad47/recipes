# Goat Pulao Recipe

Shahlla aunty's goat pulao recipe.

## Pre-Cook Steps
* Soak 3-cups of rice in water for 30 mins

## Broth
Ingredients and Recipe for broth 

### Ingredients
1. Ginger 1 tbsp
2. Galic 1 tbsp
3. Small Cardamom 15 
4. Black Pepper 13
5. Fennel 3 tbsp
6. Coriander seeds 3 tbsp
7. Onion 1 Medium
8. Salt 1 tsp
9. Baby goat 1 kg (Dasti and Seena ka gosht)

### Recipe
1. In a pot add meat with 4 glasses of water and add the following ingredients to it inside a small piece of cloth.

- Cardamom
- Black Pepper
- Fennel
- Coriander 

Cook at low flame until meat is tender (approx 1 hour)

## Rice Bagarna

## Ingredients
1. Onions 4 daali 
2. Ginger 1 tbsp
3. Garlic 1 tbsp
4. Small Cardamom 3
5. Bay leave 2
6. Green Chilli 6
8. Black Cumin Seed 1/2 tsp
9. Salt 3 tsp
10. Big Cardamom 2
11. Cloves 10
12. Oil/Ghee 1/2 Cup
13. Cinnamon 1 Inch
14. White Cumin Seed 1 tsp
15. Yogurt 2 tbsp
16. Black Pepper 13
17. Star Anise 4

## Recipe 
1. Remove meat from the broth and strain the broth 
2. Add onions to a pot and fry in ghee until gold brown
3. Remove more than half of the fried onions and spread on paper to dry
4. Add everything to the pot with the remaining onions and fry for a few minutes 
5. Add meat and fry for another few minutes
6. Add the strained broth (which should be 6 cups, if not then add water)
7. Once the mixture comes to boil add the strained rice 
8. Cook on high heat until it starts drying
9. Cook on low flame once water starts drying

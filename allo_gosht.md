# Allo Gosht
Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8XgBkJLQy7k

## Ingrediants
* 1/2 kg Beef/Mutton/Chicken -- little big 
* 1/2 cup cooking oil
* one medium/small onion
* 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
* Kharay Masalay: (Corse Grind)
    - 2 Big Elachi
    - 2 Choti Elachi
    - 1 cinammon stick
    - 1 Badwan ka phool broken
    - 1 bay leaf
    - 1 tea spoon zeera
    - 10 black pepper
    - 5 Longs
* 1 tsp laal mirch  (bhar kay)
* 1 tsp dhanya powder (bhar kay)
* 1/2 tsp haldi powder
* 1 tsp namak
* 1/2 tsp zeera powder
* 1/2 cup yougut
* 250 gram allo cut and soaked
* 2-3 drops kewra essans
* 1 tsp kasoori methi
* 4 green chillis

## Steps
1. Heat half cup cooking oil
2. Add onions and fry until golden brown and remove (add 2 goont water and make into a paste)
3. Add the meat and fry until it changes its color (4-6 mins)
4. Add 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste and the corse garam masala and bun for 2 mins
5. Add remaining masala (laal, dhanya powder, haldi, namak, zeera powder) and some water and bun for 2 mins
6. Reduce flame to dham level and add 1/2 cup yougut and cook until yougut water dry
7. Once dahi ka pani drys out (still waterry salan) set flame back to medium and add onion paste and add 1/2 cup water and cook on low heat under tender
8. Set flame to medium and add potatos and kewra essans and kasoori methi and bun 
9. add 1.25 cup of water (up to you) and cover and cook until allo tender
10. Set flame to high and add green chillis, evapourate water to desired consitancey (3 mins)

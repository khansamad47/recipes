# Daal Recipe

## Ingredients 
1. 2 cups of Dal (1cup moong + 1 cup red)
2. 1/2 teaspoon haldi
3. 1.5 teaspoon laal mirch 
4. 2 teaspoon salt
5. 1/2 onion
6. 1/4 cup oil
7. 1/2 tsp zeera
8. 2 small tomatoes
9. 1/2 teaspoon garlic
10. 2-3 green chills

## Steps
1. Wash 2 cups of daal three times
2. Heat 4 cups of water add haldi, laal mirch, salt, 1/4 chopped onion
3. Add daal and cook on slow heat for 30 mins. 
4. Grid it with spoon add water to your desired consistence level and boil for 3 mins
5. Turn off stove

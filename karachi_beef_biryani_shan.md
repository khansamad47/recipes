# Karachi Beef Biryani (Shan Modified)

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UeLdud8RWbA&ab_channel=DuakaKitchen

## Ingrediates
* 1 Kg Beef Meat on Bone
* Oil 1 1/2 Cup
* 2 Large Onions + 1 More for Garnish
* 250 grams tomatoes
* 1 1/2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
* 10 green chilies
* 1/2 kg potatoes
* 1kg rice

## Steps
1. Add onions and fry 
2. REMOVE onions for garnish
3. Add chopped Tomatoes and mix for 2-3 mins
4. Add ginger garlic paste and mix
5. Add meat and mix
6. Add 2 tbsp Daniya Powdina and mix
7. Add Karachi Beef biryani masala mix
8. Add 10 green chilles slit and mix
9. Add 5 glasses for water and let it cook for 1 1/2 hours
